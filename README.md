Project Overview: This project focuses on analyzing marketing campaign performance using three CSV datasets and a single interactive Power BI dashboard. The objective is to evaluate campaign effectiveness, customer engagement, and return on investment (ROI) to support data-driven marketing decisions.

Datasets Used (3 CSV Files):
Campaign Data – Contains campaign details such as campaign name, type, cost, and duration.
Customer / Lead Data – Includes customer demographics, leads generated, and conversions.
Sales / Revenue Data – Tracks revenue generated from campaigns and customer purchases.

Data Preparation:
Cleaned missing and duplicate values
Created relationships using a star schema model
Added calculated DAX measures for KPIs like:
Total Leads
Total Conversions
Conversion Rate (%)
Campaign Cost
Revenue
ROI (%)

Dashboard Features:
KPI Cards: Total Leads, Conversions, Revenue, ROI
Bar & Column Charts: Campaign-wise and Channel-wise performance
Line Chart: Campaign trend over time
Slicers: Campaign Type, Channel, Date


Key Insights:
Identifies top-performing campaigns and channels
Compares cost vs revenue to evaluate ROI
Helps marketing teams optimize budget allocation
Highlights campaigns with high conversion but low cost

Tools & Skills Used:
Power BI
DAX Measures
Data Modeling
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Business Insight Generation

Business Value:
This dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand which marketing campaigns deliver the highest ROI and where marketing spend should be optimized for better performance.
